# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and kind
- Welcome different perspectives
- Accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive comments
- Any form of abuse or threats

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers will review and respond to all reports.

Thank you for helping make this community welcoming! ❤️
